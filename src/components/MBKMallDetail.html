Project Overview

MBKMall is a comprehensive shopping mall application designed to enhance the shopping experience at MBK Center, one of Thailand's most popular shopping destinations. The app provides visitors with an interactive store directory, real-time promotions, and advanced navigation features.

Built with Flutter and powered by GetX state management and Firebase backend, the application delivers a seamless shopping experience with personalized recommendations and location-based services.
